About PHPKonf

PHPKonf is hosted by the İstanbul PHP community (İstanbul PHP) in İstanbul, Turkey
from May 20-21st 2018, and you're invited! For the 5th year, we'll host some of the best speakers, awesome talk topics,
latest technologies, and up to date news in PHP.
